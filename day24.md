### Q2
Anamika, Gopal, Nischal, Vanmayi, Shresta, and Sripad are sitting in a straight line facing north.  
Nischal is to the immediate left of Shresta.  
Anamika and Sripad are seated second from the right end.  
Four persons are sitting between Nischal and Vanmayi.  
If Gopal and Sripad interchange their positions, **who will be sitting between Vanmayi and Shresta?**



### Q3

Anubhav is sitting immediately left of Saif.  
Only one person is sitting between Piyush and Ashutosh.  
**Who is sitting to the immediate left of Ashutosh?**
